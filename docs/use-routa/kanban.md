# Kanban

Kanban is Routa's workflow-driven execution mode.

## What Kanban Is For

Choose Kanban when you want:

- explicit stages such as backlog, todo, dev, review, and done
- visible delivery flow instead of one freeform execution thread
- automation triggered by card movement and lane policy

## How It Works

Kanban is not just a board view. In Routa, lane transitions can create sessions, route work to
specialists, and enforce delivery checks for stages like `review` and `done`.

## Best Fit

Kanban works best when:

- work needs a stable handoff process
- you want review and done to act as real gates
- multiple tasks should move through the same delivery model

## What Makes It Different

The orchestration boundary in Kanban is the card and its lane state, not one conversation. That
lets Routa apply policy, queueing, and stage-specific specialist behavior at the workflow layer.
